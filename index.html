About me

My name is Yesenia Román.

I am a creative, bilingual librarian with over seven years of experience in reference, acquisition, and cataloging services at research institutions, and public libraries. I enjoy helping people conduct research, learn, and make discoveries, and believe access to information is a human right.

I have a Master's of Library and Information Sciences with a Specialization in Data Curation from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, and a B.A in Sociocultural Anthropology from the University of Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras.

In my free time, I like making things, reading, and hanging out with my two black cats, Cosmo and Nebula.
