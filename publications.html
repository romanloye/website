Publications

In my career as a librarian, I’ve had the opportunity to provide specialized, in-depth data support to researchers and scholars in different capacities. Whether it is by strategizing a systematic review or scoping data availability worldwide for a specific project, finding the needle in the stack is something I enjoy. My work with researchers has led to 5 peer-reviewed publications in high-impact journals, such as The Lancet.
